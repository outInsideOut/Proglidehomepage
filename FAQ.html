We understand that there are questions that will come naturally when thinking about your new garage door.

How Much Does an Electric Roller Garage Door Cost?

Due to all our garage doors being different, we price all our work on an individual basis. We offer free, no-obligation quotes, all of which are priced competitively.

How Long Does a Garage Door Installation Take?

When it comes to installing your new electric garage door, we'll give an estimated time frame. Our team of garage door fitters ensure an efficient installation service is provided.

Will I Get to Choose the Installation Date?

Yes, we'll arrange a day and time that's suitable for you when it comes to the installation of your new garage door.

Is It Only Electric Garage Doors You Offer?

Yes. We believe that the roller door is, overall, the best door for any garage, so why offer alternatives? This, of course, is just our opinion. You also receive a free 5-year warranty.

About Remote Roller Door Systems

Proglide Lincoln specialises in both the 55mm and 77mm lathe-remote roller door. With a fully remote roller door, you benefit from not only top security features and a stunning aesthetic, but the ability to create more space within your garage by having us to install the door in a position that suits you.
